Copier Exporters

Midwest Copier Exchange specializes in the export of copiers and printers worldwide. Our markets include South and Central America, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and Africa.We remarket every major brand of copiers and maintain a stock of over 6,000 items at our facility in Chicago Illinois.

We have over 25 years of experience in managing the logistics of moving containers around the world. Our team members are experts in the packaging and loading of containers to maximize the capacity and minimize damage to your order and a dedicated account manager will work with you through the entire process.
